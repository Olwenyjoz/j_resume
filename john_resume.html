About Me

Inspiring Minds, Building Futures

With over a decade of dedicated service in education, I have committed my professional life to nurturing the next generation of thinkers, leaders, and change-makers. My journey began at the prestigious University of Nairobi, where I graduated with First Class Honors in Education Arts in 2014.

Currently serving at Kitambo Mixed Secondary School, I specialize in History, Government, and Christian Religious Education. My teaching philosophy centers on creating engaging, thought-provoking learning environments where students can discover their passions and develop critical thinking skills.

Beyond the classroom, I am passionate about mentorship, believing that education extends far beyond academic achievement to encompass character development and life skills.
